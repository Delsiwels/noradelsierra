# Month-End Close Procedures

## Standard Month-End Checklist

### 1. Bank Reconciliation
- Reconcile all bank accounts to bank statements
- Investigate and resolve any outstanding items
- Ensure all transactions are coded correctly

### 2. Accounts Receivable
- Review aged receivables report
- Follow up overdue invoices
- Assess need for doubtful debt provisions

### 3. Accounts Payable
- Process all outstanding supplier invoices
- Review aged payables report
- Ensure all bills are coded to correct periods

### 4. GST Reconciliation
- Reconcile GST collected vs GST paid
- Ensure all transactions have correct GST treatment
- Review BAS-excluded items

### 5. Payroll
- Verify all pay runs processed correctly
- Reconcile payroll clearing accounts
- Confirm superannuation payments are up to date

### 6. Credit Cards
- Reconcile all business credit card statements
- Ensure all transactions are receipted and coded

### 7. Adjusting Entries
- Process depreciation entries
- Adjust prepaid expenses
- Process accruals for expenses incurred but not billed

### 8. Review Reports
- Review Profit & Loss for unusual items
- Review Balance Sheet for accuracy
- Compare to budget and prior periods

## Common Issues
- Unreconciled bank transactions from prior months
- Missing supplier invoices (check for statements)
- Incorrect GST coding on transactions
- Timing differences between payment and invoice dates
- Forgetting to reverse prior month accruals
