# BAS Deadline Information

## Quarterly BAS Due Dates (Standard)
- **Q1 (Jul-Sep)**: Due 28 October
- **Q2 (Oct-Dec)**: Due 28 February
- **Q3 (Jan-Mar)**: Due 28 April
- **Q4 (Apr-Jun)**: Due 28 July (if lodging electronically via a registered tax agent, may get extra time)

## Monthly BAS Due Dates
- Due on the 21st of the following month
- Example: January BAS due 21 February

## Electronic Lodgement
- Tax agents may receive a 4-week deferral for electronic lodgement
- Check the ATO registered agent portal for specific due dates

## Late Lodgement Penalties
- Failure to Lodge (FTL) penalty: 1 penalty unit for each 28-day period (or part thereof) that the BAS is overdue
- Maximum penalty: 5 penalty units
- Current penalty unit value: check ATO website for current rates
- The ATO may remit penalties in certain circumstances

## Payment Options
- BPAY using the payment reference number (PRN) on your BAS
- Direct debit
- Credit card (via third-party providers)
- Payment plans available if unable to pay in full

## Tips
- Lodge on time even if you cannot pay to avoid the FTL penalty
- Set up reminders at least 2 weeks before due dates
- Keep records for at least 5 years
- If using a BAS agent, ensure they have your records well before the due date
